The Chicken Brothers Drive Tru
The Chicken Brothers Drive Thru was the first time I used lists to their full effect. My idea was if I had two lists I could append them at the same time and once the drive thru was done, I could print an entire receipt with the bought items on one side, then the cost of the item on the other side. And be able to print the total cost plus tax. This project taught me a lot about how lists work, I would’ve never been able to make my Dungeon Crawler game if this project never happened.
RPS Tornament
In the Rock Paper Scisors Tornement, I first experimented with functions but didn’t understand them fully yet so I decided to store values into a variable. I called this variable ‘strat’ my first goal was to beat two teams, simple enough. After I was able to flawless two teams I wanted to keep going and coded strategies for all preset teams, then the day of the tournament. I don’t recall who won, but I know I won the second tournement because I coded a separate strategy to counter everyone else’s strategies.
